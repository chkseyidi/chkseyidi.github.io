About

Engineering for the languages everyone else skips.

I'm an AI engineer based in Nouakchott, working across Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Multi-Agent Systems — with a focus on building NLP and autonomous agents for languages with very little digital footprint.

My work spans custom embeddings, semantic search with FAISS, vision models, and multi-agent orchestration with planning, tool-use, and self-correction loops. I care about quality, scalability, and validation — the dull parts that decide whether an AI system actually survives contact with real users.
